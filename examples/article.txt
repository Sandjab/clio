On Tuesday, Microsoft announced a partnership with OpenAI to expand their joint AI research facility in Seattle. The deal, valued at $10 billion, was confirmed by CEO Satya Nadella during a press conference in Redmond. Sam Altman of OpenAI commented that the collaboration would extend their joint work on large language models. The partnership officially begins on March 1, 2026, with Anthropic separately commenting from San Francisco that it sees the move as healthy for the broader AI ecosystem.
